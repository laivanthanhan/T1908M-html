NOUNS

A noun represents a person, place, animal, thing or an idea

KINDS OF NOUN

classified as follows:

Proper: It is the name of a particular object,whether person,place,animal,idea or thing.

Common: It the name possessed by thing in general

Gollective: It is the name representing a group of things.

Abstract: It is the name represeting qualities,emotions,conditions or actions.

PRONOUNS

A pronoun is a word used in place of a noun.A pronoun is generally used to avoid repetition of a noun.

KINDS OF PRONOUN

Pronouns are classified as follows:

Personal pronouns: It refers to a specific noun and used as a substitute of the noun

Interrogative pronoun: It stands as the answer to the question about a person, place or a thing.

Indefinite pronoun: It is used to refer to a person, thing of a place that are unknown.

Relative pronouns: It relates to a noun, pronoun, word of expression preceding it n the sentence, and at the same time connects tow statements.
